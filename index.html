Product Features

We only provide the finest quality potatoes grown on organic only farms.

Any potatoes that fail to make the cut are recycled.

Our potatoes are delivered to our customers within 3 working days to ensure full freshness.

We also offer a next day guarantee delivery that can be made upon request.

Every bag of potatoes we deliver to our customers can be traced back to the exact field in that it was grown.

Further information on the farming methods used can be sent upon request free of charge
